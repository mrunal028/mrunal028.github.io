Bio

I am Dr. Mrunal Reddy Ragi who is a highly skilled Health Data Scientist with a strong background in dentistry, blending clinical expertise with advanced data analytics to drive insights in healthcare. Holding a Master’s degree in Health Data Science from Saint Louis University (GPA: 3.55/4.00) and a Bachelor’s degree in Dental Science from Sri Sai College of Dental Surgery (GPA: 3.00/4.00), I posses a unique ability to bridge the gap between healthcare and data-driven decision-making. Proficient in Python, R, SQL, and SAS, specializes in statistical modeling, machine learning, and healthcare analytics. With hands-on experience in tools like Tableau, Power BI, Azure Data Factory, and TensorFlow. My technical expertise extends to cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure, as well as database management systems such as Snowflake, PostgreSQL, and Amazon Redshift.

In my professional journey, I have served as a Dentist at Facial Aesthetic Studio, assessed over 350 patients, performed cosmetic treatments, and maintained a 96.6% patient satisfaction rate. my experience also includes a dental internship at Sri Sai College of Dental Surgery, where I honed my skills in patient care, clinical documentation, and adherence to infection control protocols. I am passionate about leveraging data to improve healthcare outcomes and has participated in various symposiums and competitions, including the 5th Telangana State Dental Conference and a national-level e-Poster competition. My work is driven by a commitment to compassionate care, strategic problem-solving, and continuous learning.
